位於大埔滘互動自然中心內的小白鷺餐廳，面向吐露港，背倚翠綠群山。餐廳迴廊外就是小白鷺瀉湖，湖中常有小白鷺徜徉飛翔。整個建築物採用寧靜雅緻的十九世紀南歐建築風格設計，室內裝飾則混合了原始色彩和異國情調，環境質樸舒適，讓您可以邊享用美食，邊神馳於令人神往的大自然氛圍中。

Environment
Named after the flock of little egrets feeding regularly in the lagoon outside the restaurant, the a Little Egret Restaurant is a haven for the busy Hong Kong people seeking refuge to recharge their batteries. The Nature Centre where the restaurant is housed is a two-leveled 19th Century Mediterranean style building. The elegant, laid back and relaxed tone of this building. Blends effortlessly with the idyllic surrounding.


 


小白鷺餐廳佔地萬多呎，可同時為三百多位顧客提供服務。內設有地窖吧、玻里尼西亞角落、發現號兒童探險船、小木偶角及音樂演奏台。在特別的日子更會為您準備清新脫俗的各類輕音樂演奏。 另外迴廊雅座、星光露天酒廊及宴會廳，又是您的另一種選擇。

Facilities
The interior decorations of the restaurant are eclectic blends of elements from diverse primitive and exotic cultures. You can find Polynesian tapa lying side by side with American Native pottery, an East African Maasai tribal sculpture is merged harmoniously with Mayan fabric.

The 6,000 square feet floor space contains more than 300 seats, a fully licensed bar, a Polynesian corner, and the Kid's corner with "The Discovery" - an exploratory ship for children. The stage is decorated in Taos / Santa Fe style. This will be the centre for a variety of performances during special occasions. Alternatively, you can take a seat in our verandah and starry night tavern or hold a party in one of our function rooms.


 

 


小白鷺餐廳由行政總廚至每一位廚師都是美食家和經驗豐富的烹調藝術家。他們精心泡製世界各地的名餚，例如法式焗田螺、燒非洲雞、 墨西哥烤雞卷、意式生牛肉片、夏威夷大蝦、泰式咖喱…..，迎接您的光臨。 中心內有專人打理的香草菜園，出品日日新鮮，更富營養，更符合您的口味。

Gourmet Cuisine
Our experienced kitchen staffs are on hand to prepare rich and delicious cuisines from around the world for our patrons.
